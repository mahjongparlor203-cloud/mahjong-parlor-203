A Boutique Mahjong Lounge

Mahjong Parlor 203 offers lessons, guided play, and relaxed open play in a welcoming social setting.

The Parlor Experience

Mahjong Parlor 203 is designed to feel like a refined private lounge — a place where friends gather, new players learn the game, and experienced players enjoy thoughtful competition.

Snacks and non‑alcoholic beverages are provided at all sessions so players can relax and enjoy the game.

🀄 Lessons

Build confidence, sharpen your strategy, and learn Mahjong in a polished, encouraging environment.

Lesson Format

2–3 hour sessions — $125 per person

Sessions can also be split into three 2‑hour classes if preferred.

🀄Mahj 101

Perfect for students brand new to Mahjong. Learn how to identify tiles and suits, read the National Mah Jongg League (NMJL) card, set up the table, and understand the flow of play.

🀄Mahj 102

For beginners who know the basics. Students focus on rules, Charleston strategy, and building hands while learning to read the NMJL card more fluently.

At least one full round of Mahjong will be completed.

🀄 Guided Play

Play full Mahjong games with light instructor guidance. Guided play helps players practice the deal, Charleston, committing to hands, remembering rules, and learning defensive play.

1–2 hour session — $50 per person.

🀄 Charleston Strategy

Take the stress out of the Charleston and learn to “play messy.” Students will create random hands and practice what to pass, what to keep, and what hands to commit to.

Players will then play full games and also learn solo mahj techniques for practicing at home.

1–2 hour session — $50 per person.

🀄 Open Play

Grab a seat, reserve a table, or organize multiple tables for a relaxed Mahjong gathering.

An instructor will be present to answer questions and keep games moving, though strategy coaching is not provided during open play sessions.

1–2 hour session — $25 per person.
